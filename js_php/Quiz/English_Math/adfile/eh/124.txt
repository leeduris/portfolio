<h1>Describe a Picture</h1>














Intro:
This is a picture of a busy street.

Main Focus:
In the middle of the picture, two street cars are running on the rails.
A lot of people are walking along both sides of the street.
All of them are wearing summer clothes, such as T-shirt and sleeveless shirts.

Sub Focus:
The buildings look very classic and sign boards are hanging on the buildings.
A big yellow banner is hanging at the upper side of the picture.

Closing:
It is a very sunny day since I can see plenty of sunshine in this picture.
