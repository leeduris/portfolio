This picture is taken at a runway.
There is a private plane which is a white and a blue stripe in the center of the plane.
Three people are standing in line to get on the plane.
The woman at the front of the line is carrying a white bag and walking up the airplane boarding ladder and two other men holding big bags are following her.
They must be attending a very important business meeting because all of them are wearing nice business suits.
